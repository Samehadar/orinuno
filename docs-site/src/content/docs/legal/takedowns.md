title: Takedown Requests
description: How rights holders and platform representatives can request adjustments or removals.
---

If you are a rights holder, platform representative, or any other party
with a legitimate concern about content in this repository — including but
not limited to code that facilitates infringement, vendor documentation,
test fixtures, or screenshots — please get in touch through one of the
channels below.

## Channels

1. Open a public GitHub issue at <https://github.com/Samehadar/orinuno/issues>
   with a `[takedown]` prefix in the title. That is the fastest path.
2. Contact the maintainer privately via the contact links on
   <https://lyutarevich.com/>.

## What to expect

Reasonable requests will be reviewed and acted upon in good faith. The
project is maintained by a single person, so best-effort timing is:

- Acknowledgement within 7 days.
- Initial review and either action or a clarifying question within 14
  days.
